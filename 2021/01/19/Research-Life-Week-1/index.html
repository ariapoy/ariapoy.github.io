Research Life Week 1

Created2021-01-19|Updated2021-01-20|Research

First Group Meeting

I’m a little nervous about speaking without English for a long time.

Impression of Members in CLLab

Si-An: Kind with humble. He introduces the CLLab to me first. And give lots of tips to join and do the research. Hsuan-Tien says Si-An has cultivated the skills of doing research.
Wei-I: Smart and patient. He brings some ideas of LLP and optimization theory. It is exciting.
Ching-Yuan: Genius with aggressive. He directly asks the core problems of my presentation with fluent English. And provide useful information on related work and researcher on the ML in crowdsourcing field.
Ashesh: Mature dreamer. He shares the work, research, and life experience with me. We could feel the same feeling after working for several years. Both of us are dreamers.

There are currently 14 members (including me) in CLLab. All of the members have diversity personality.

First Personal Meeting

Impression of Teacher in CLLab

Hsuan-Tien is a fantastic teacher!

  • His strong of machine learning and sensitivity, the experience of ML problems.
  • His patient and cultivated for meeting with the students.

I’ll give some comments to you. But it would be best if you determined the suitable way to absorb these ideas. And take responsibility for the good as well as bad results.

H.T. is the doctor!

  • The goal of the meeting is to help students.
  • As see a doctor, the various patient has a different time.

Keep the weekly meeting as a habit. And you could get more if you need it.

  • In the future, I’ll keep learning and learning with some vocation.
