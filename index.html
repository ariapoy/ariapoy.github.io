Aria's Memo

Po-Yi Lu (呂栢頤)

I am going to be a Ph. D. student in the Graduate Institute of Networking and Multimedia (GINM) at National Taiwan University from 2021. I also a researcher in Big Data Laboratory at Chunghwa Telecom Laboratories (CHTTL). Before joining GINM and CHTTL, I received my M.S. degree at National Taiwan University under supervision of Prof. Argon Chen. My research interests include:

  • Data Quality Management
  • Weakly Supervised Learning
  • Ensemble Learning
  • Text Mining and Natural Language Processing

Please see my CV for more information.
